Primary Health Centre (PHC)

Details: The Primary Health Centre in Bamkheda provides essential healthcare services. It has an Outpatient Department (OPD), maternal and child care services, immunization drives, and first aid facilities. Staff includes one Medical Officer, Community Health Officers, ANM nurses, and ASHA workers covering all village households. The PHC also conducts regular health camps and awareness programs.

×

Water Supply

Details: Bamkheda village has multiple overhead tanks and borewells managed by the Panchayat. More than 700 households receive piped drinking water connections. Daily distribution is monitored by dedicated staff to ensure clean and safe water. The Panchayat has also installed a Methan Plant for efficient waste-water management and recycling.

×

Sanitation & Waste Management

Details: The Panchayat has ensured 100% toilet coverage across all 713 households. Public toilets are maintained for travelers and gatherings. Waste is collected daily using bins placed in key village areas, with proper disposal and composting practices. Regular cleanliness drives are held to maintain hygiene and spread awareness under the Swachh Bharat Mission.

×

Post Office & Banking Services

Details: Bamkheda has a local Post Office for postal services, money orders, and savings accounts. Residents also have access to nearby cooperative banks and nationalized banks with ATM facilities. Farmers benefit from Kisan Credit Cards and rural banking initiatives. Digital banking services are also being introduced for ease of transactions.

×

Community Facilities

Details: The Panchayat maintains a multipurpose Community Hall used for cultural programs, Gram Sabha meetings, and public gatherings. A dedicated playground is available for schoolchildren and youth sports activities. Additional facilities include seating areas, small libraries, and spaces for women’s self-help groups. Festivals and national celebrations are organized here, strengthening social bonds in the village.
